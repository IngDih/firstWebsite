In this project I thought of using bootstrap or other templates I could find online to make this look more advanced and professional, but decided to make it all by my own, from scratch, utilizing some research mainly from W3Schools, that helped a lot to put things together.

The overall concept of this website was to get out of the conventional black, gray and white to make a girly template, with pastel colours, round borders and a neutral white background, that would aim an arts and craft public, and serve as a good template for a bullet journal website. My inspiration was probably the style of template I would liked to have had on my tumblr in 2017, when I was desperate to find something cute and round like those flip cellphones or old typing machines.

All the pictures used that were colected from the internet and saved on the img folder were referenced on the "references" text document, as well as the video embed on the index page.
